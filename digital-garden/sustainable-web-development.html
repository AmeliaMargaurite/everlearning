Sustainable Web Development

So technically I haven't even finished setting up this site, but I have to go ahead and start writing now. I've been working on expanding my self-education around all things web development/internet/life, and today stumbled onto this post from A List Apart Sustainable Web Design, An Excerpt. I will admit that sustainability regarding the internet isn't something I think of often. I have heard in passing of the hidden environmental costs involved with browsing the web, however I honestly never thought too much about it. I'm going to make sure that changes.

Within this excerpt from A List Apart, Tom mentions this tool, Website Carbon Calculator, his team has built to assess a websites carbon emissions, and the form of energy the hosting service uses. The website I am currently building has scored well in terms of cleanliness of the site. Unfortunately the hosting for the site is using, in their terms, 'bog standard energy'. Which in turn has prompted me to research more into sustainable hosting.
